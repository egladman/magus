      Search Syntax

      Terms are combined with AND and matched case-insensitively. Click an example to run it.

      tag:build
      Pages carrying the exact tag "build".
      -tag:spell
      Exclude pages with a tag.
      tag:"remote cache"
      Quote values that contain spaces.
      title:install
      Scope a term to one field: title, description, or text.
      "exact phrase"
      Match words that appear together, in order.
      cache -go
      Require a word; drop another with a leading minus.
      tag:cache OR tag:build
      OR matches either; terms are ANDed by default.
      tag:(cache OR build)
      Group several values for one field.
      title:mag*
      Wildcards: * stands for any run of characters.
      (tag:a OR tag:b) -tag:c
      Parenthesize to group; combine with exclusion.
